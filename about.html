About me

Professional experience

I started programming when I was in 10th grade. We had a computer science class and we had to learn to code in C++ by using a self-teaching website. It didn't take long before I started thoroughly enjoying programming. However, at that time, I struggled with understanding many general concepts, most notably object-oriented programming, so I moved on to learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Through a monstrous amount of practice and countless projects of various scales, I started understanding the concepts that challenged me the most and gained more confidence in my own abilities, which allowed me to move beyond the world of front-end programming.

Education

I graduated from First Secondary School "Pencho Slaveykov" with Spanish with an excellent grade. During my high school years, I was a member of the school's debates club where I had the opportunity to debate on various contemporary topics and often in favor of a side that I did not agree with personally. I have participated in various olympiads, reaching second round of olympiads in English, Spanish, history, and philosophy.

I am currently studying for a bachelor's degree in marketing at the University of National and World Economics.

Personal life

When I am not programming, I enjoy playing various sports, such as soccer and table tennis. I am also particularly fond of chess. I have a deep interest in history and geography, though I don't shy away from other fields if I find something interesting to learn about.
